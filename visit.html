--- layout: default title: Visit Radlands description: Visit Radlands skate Plaza in Northampton. Find where we are located. ---

Location: Off Bedford Road, Northampton, NN1 5PZ Opposite the Ford Garage

Opening Times: The Plaza is free to enter and is open all day every day - as long as the weather and day light permits. In the evening when the light is low, the Plaza is floodlit, the lights are turned off at 9:30pm.

Contact: If you wish to get in contact with us or you would like to get some media added, or basically anything, get in touch via radlandsplaza@gmail.com
